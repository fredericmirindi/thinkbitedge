ThinkBitEdge: Bridging the Gap

How we are reshaping AI literacy in Canada.

Our Core Mission

Technology is the great equalizer, but only if access is universal.

AI Literacy

Demystifying neural networks, data science, and automation tools for non-technical audiences.

Inclusivity

Specifically designing curriculums for BIPOC, Indigenous peoples, and underrepresented youth across Canada.

Future Readiness

Preparing the next generation of Canadian workforce for the demands of the algorithmic economy.

Why We Started

ThinkBitEdge Corp. was founded on the belief that the tools of tomorrow should not be reserved for the few. As AI reshapes industries, the "literacy gap" threatens to leave entire communities behind.

We are here to ensure that the powerful tools of Machine Learning and Data Science are placed in the hands of diverse innovators who will build a more equitable future.

Educational Programs

Hands-on workshops designed to turn curiosity into capability.

Level 1 Introductory

AI for Everyone: The Basics

A non-coding introduction to what AI is, how it works, and its ethical implications. Perfect for high school students and community leaders.

  • Understanding LLMs
  • Prompt Engineering
  • AI Ethics
Level 2 Intermediate

Data Science Bootcamp

A hands-on coding workshop using Python. Students learn to visualize data and build their first predictive models using real-world datasets relevant to their communities.

  • Python Basics
  • Pandas & Matplotlib
  • Simple Regression
Level 3 Advanced

Machine Learning & Edge AI

For advanced learners, we explore deploying AI models on small devices ("Edge Computing"). Bridging the gap between software and hardware.

  • Scikit-Learn
  • Neural Networks
  • IoT Integration
