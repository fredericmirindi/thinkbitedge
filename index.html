About ThinkBit Edge Corp

Bridging the digital divide through inclusive technology education

Since 2018, we've been dedicated to empowering youth from underrepresented groups by providing equitable access to high-quality education in emerging technologies, including artificial intelligence.

🎯

Our Mission

ThinkBit Edge Corp. is dedicated to empowering youth, especially those from underrepresented groups, by providing equitable access to high-quality education in new technologies, including artificial intelligence (AI). Our purpose is to inspire, educate, and support the next generation of innovators by making learning opportunities in emerging tech accessible, engaging, and inclusive.

🔮

Our Vision

To bridge the digital divide and foster a diverse community of future leaders equipped with the skills and confidence to thrive in the rapidly evolving world of technology.

Our Story

Founded in 2018 by Dr. Sarah Chen after recognizing the significant lack of diversity in AI and technology fields during her time at Google, ThinkBit Edge Corp was born from a vision to create lasting change in technology education.

What started as a small pilot program has grown into a comprehensive educational initiative that has touched the lives of thousands of students across the country. Our journey reflects our unwavering commitment to breaking down barriers and creating opportunities for all young people to participate in the digital revolution.

Our Programs

Comprehensive technology education designed for tomorrow's innovators

AI Foundations Bootcamp

8 weeks

8-week intensive program for high school students covering AI fundamentals, machine learning, and hands-on projects

Target: High school students (ages 16-18)
Requirements: No prior coding experience required

Program Features:

  • Hands-on AI projects
  • Industry mentorship
  • Career guidance
  • Certificate completion

Code Craft Academy

12 weeks

Beginner-friendly coding workshops focusing on web development and mobile app creation

Target: Middle & high school students (ages 13-18)
Requirements: Basic computer skills

Program Features:

  • Web development
  • Mobile app creation
  • Portfolio development
  • Peer collaboration

Future Leaders Mentorship

6 months

One-on-one professional guidance connecting students with industry professionals

Target: High school & college students
Requirements: Application and interview process

Program Features:

  • 1-on-1 mentoring
  • Industry connections
  • Leadership development
  • Career planning

Community Tech Outreach

Ongoing

Mobile tech labs bringing programming and AI education directly to underserved communities

Target: All ages
Requirements: Community partnership

Program Features:

  • Mobile labs
  • On-site workshops
  • Teacher training
  • Equipment loans
